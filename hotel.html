ABOUT

Welcome to Seaside Retreat!

Our hotel is a cozy and comfortable haven located in the heart of a picturesque beach town. We've been welcoming guests since 1995 and pride ourselves on providing exceptional service and a relaxing atmosphere.

Mission and Values

At Seaside Retreat, our mission is to provide guests with a memorable and enjoyable experience. We believe in going above and beyond to make our guests feel at home and ensuring they have everything they need to have a comfortable and enjoyable stay. We're committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. We use energy-efficient lighting, encourage guests to reuse towels, and source local and organic ingredients for our restaurant.

Amenities and features

Seaside Retreat offers a range of amenities and features to make your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. These include:

Complimentary continental breakfast

Outdoor pool and hot tub

Fitness Center

On-site restaurant serving fresh, locally sourced cuisine

Complimentary Wifi throughout the hotel

Pet-friendly accomodations

Our Rooms

At Sunset View Hotel, we offer a range of comfortable and stylish rooms to suit every guest's needs. Each of our rooms is designed with your comfort in mind, and our attention to detail ensures that you'll feel right at home during your stay.

Standard Room

Our Standard Room is perfect for travelers looking for a comfortable and affordable option. This room features a cozy queen-size bed with plush bedding, a 32-inch flat-screen TV, a mini-fridge, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower.

Deluxe Room

For guests looking for a bit more space and luxury, our Deluxe Room is an excellent choice. This room features a king-size bed with premium bedding, a comfortable seating area, a 42-inch flat-screen TV, a mini-fridge, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and separate soaking tub.

Families or groups of friends will appreciate our spacious Family Room. This room features two queen-size beds with comfortable bedding, a 42-inch flat-screen TV, a mini-fridge, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower.

Family Room

Our Junior Suite is the perfect choice for guests looking for a little extra space and comfort. This room features a king-size bed with premium bedding, a separate seating area with a sofa bed, a 42-inch flat-screen TV, a mini-fridge, and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and separate soaking tub.

Junior Suite

All of our rooms feature free Wi-Fi, individual climate control, a work desk, and complimentary toiletries. Plus, all guests can enjoy access to our on-site fitness center, business center, and rooftop terrace with stunning views of the city.
